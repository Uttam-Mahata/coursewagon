Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

1. Introduction

CourseWagon ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our website https://coursewagon.live.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile data when you sign up.
  • Gemini API Key: This key is encrypted before being stored in our database and decrypted only when generating content at your request.
  • Usage Data: Information like your activity logs, content creation history, and interaction time.
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, OS, and access timestamps.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our platform features.
  • To personalize the experience based on your usage patterns.
  • To generate content via Gemini API using your key, securely decrypted at runtime.
  • To detect and prevent security threats and unauthorized access.
  • To send administrative messages, including feature updates or policy changes.

4. Data Security

Your Gemini API key is stored in encrypted form. All data transmission is secured with HTTPS. While we apply best security practices, no online service is completely risk-free.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as your account is active or as required by legal obligations. You may delete your account and data by contacting support.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data. You can manage this via your profile settings or request it by email.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services (e.g., Google Gemini API) to deliver core functionality. These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review them before use.

8. Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your data where required to comply with legal obligations, such as court orders, or to enforce our Terms of Service.

9. Children's Privacy

CourseWagon is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page and, if significant, we may notify you via email or site notice.

12. Contact
