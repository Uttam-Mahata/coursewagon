Your Journey to Creating Courses

Four simple steps to transform your ideas into structured educational content

1

Author Your Course

Begin your course creation journey by entering a course description. Type your course topic in the input field, or use the innovative voice recording feature to describe your course naturally.

Pro Tip:

Use the "Generate Course from Text" button for quick AI-powered content generation, or record your voice for a more personalized touch!

2

Organize Your Content

After generating your course, our AI organizes it into structured subjects. Navigate to "My Courses" to view all your created courses and access their detailed content structure.

What's Included:

Each course is automatically structured with relevant subjects, making it easy to manage and navigate your educational content.

3

Explore Subjects

Dive deep into each subject to explore the comprehensive topics generated for your course. View beautifully designed subject cards with AI-generated images and organized content modules.

Features:

  • • AI-generated subject thumbnails
  • • Edit and update subjects on the fly
  • • Add new subjects to expand your course
  • • Intuitive "View Content" buttons for easy navigation
4

Access Rich Content

Click on any subject to access detailed, AI-generated educational content. Each topic is enriched with comprehensive explanations, examples, and structured learning materials.

Content Management:

  • • Edit and customize content to match your teaching style
  • • Add new chapters and topics dynamically
  • • Update existing content with real-time changes
  • • Navigate through course outline in the sidebar

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI generate course content?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing powered by Google Gemini to analyze your course description and generate structured, relevant educational content tailored to your topic.

Can I edit the generated content?

Absolutely! All content is fully editable. You can modify subjects, chapters, topics, and content to match your specific teaching style and requirements.

What formats can I export my courses to?

Currently, courses are stored in our cloud platform with access through your dashboard. We're working on export features for PDF and other formats in future updates.

Is there a limit to how many courses I can create?

Free accounts can create unlimited courses. We're committed to making quality education accessible to all educators.
