Getting Started

You can create an account in two ways:

  1. Email/Password: Click "Sign Up" on the authentication page, fill in your first name, last name, email, and password, then submit.
  2. Google Sign-In: Click "Sign up with Google" and authorize with your Google account for instant access.

After registration, you'll be automatically logged in and can start creating courses.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and enter your new password
  5. Confirm the new password and submit

The reset link expires after a certain time for security reasons. If it expires, request a new one.

Course Wagon has a simple navigation structure:

  • Home: Landing page with platform overview
  • Create Course: AI-powered course generation tool
  • My Courses: View and manage all your created courses
  • Profile: Update your account details and change password
  • How It Works: Step-by-step guide to using the platform

Course Authoring

Our AI-powered course generator uses Google Gemini AI to:

  1. Analyze your course description or voice input
  2. Generate a structured course outline with subjects
  3. Create topics for each subject
  4. Generate detailed educational content for each topic
  5. Create relevant images for subjects automatically

The entire process typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on course complexity.

To create a course using voice:

  1. Click "Start Recording" button on the Create Course page
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  3. Speak clearly about your course topic and what you want to cover
  4. Click "Stop Recording" when finished
  5. Play back your recording to verify (optional)
  6. Click "Generate Course from Audio" to create your course

Pro Tip: Speak naturally and include key topics you want covered. The more detail you provide, the better the AI can structure your course.

Yes! All content is fully editable:

  • Subjects: Edit titles, descriptions, or delete entire subjects
  • Topics: Modify topic names, add new topics, or remove existing ones
  • Content: Edit the detailed content using our rich text editor
  • Images: Subject images are AI-generated but can be replaced

All changes are saved in real-time to the cloud.

Course Wagon uses a hierarchical structure:

Course ‚Üí Subject ‚Üí Topic ‚Üí Content

  • Course: The top-level container (e.g., "Introduction to Web Development")
  • Subject: Major sections of your course (e.g., "HTML Basics", "CSS Styling")
  • Topic: Specific lessons within a subject (e.g., "HTML Tags", "Semantic HTML")
  • Content: Detailed educational material for each topic

Troubleshooting

This error occurs when:

  • Your email or password is incorrect
  • Your account doesn't exist (you may need to sign up first)
  • There's a typo in your email address

Solutions:

  1. Double-check your email and password
  2. Use "Forgot password?" to reset your password
  3. Try signing in with Google if you registered that way
  4. Create a new account if you haven't registered yet

Common causes and solutions:

  1. Microphone Permission: Allow microphone access when your browser prompts you
  2. Browser Compatibility: Use Chrome, Firefox, or Edge (latest versions)
  3. HTTPS Required: Voice recording only works on secure connections
  4. Check Microphone: Ensure your microphone is connected and working
  5. Browser Settings: Check your browser's site permissions for microphone access

Your session expires after 1 hour of inactivity for security reasons.

To avoid this:

  • Check "Remember me" when logging in for extended sessions
  • Save your work regularly (content auto-saves, but it's good practice)
  • Log back in when prompted - your data is safe

Course generation typically takes 30-90 seconds. If it takes longer:

  • Don't refresh the page - generation is still in progress
  • Complex courses with many subjects take longer
  • Voice recordings take slightly longer due to audio processing
  • If it takes more than 2 minutes, there may be a server issue - try again

You'll see a loading animation while the AI works. Be patient!

Try these solutions:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Wait a few moments - images may still be generating

Subject images are generated by AI and stored in cloud storage. If issues persist, contact support.
